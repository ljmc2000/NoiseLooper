Intended to be an android port of <a href="https://github.com/rafaelmardojai/blanket">Blanket</a>.

Features:
<ul>
	<li>Play any combination of more than 12 calming noises on loop mixed at volumes of your chosing</li>
	<li>Import custom audio assets</li>
	<li>Save your favorite combinations and load them later</li>
	<li>Start a default profile on launch (optional)</li>
	<li>Set a sleep timer and have all noises fade out after a delay</li>
</ul>

Based on <a href="https://github.com/rafaelmardojai/blanket">Blanket</a>, available on <a href="https://flathub.org/apps/com.rafaelmardojai.Blanket">flathub</a>.
